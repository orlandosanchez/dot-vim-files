  <h2>Markdown Cheat Sheet</h2>

  <div class="cheatsheet-content">

  <div class="mod">
    <div class="col">
      <h3>Format Text</h3>
      <p>Headers</p>
      <pre>
# This is an &lt;h1&gt; tag
## This is an &lt;h2&gt; tag
###### This is an &lt;h6&gt; tag</pre>
     <p>Text styles</p>
     <pre>
*This text will be italic*
_This will also be italic_
**This text will be bold**
__This will also be bold__

*You **can** combine them*
</pre>
    </div>
    <div class="col">
      <h3>Lists</h3>
      <p>Unordered</p>
      <pre>
* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Item 2a
  * Item 2b</pre>
     <p>Ordered</p>
     <pre>
1. Item 1
2. Item 2
3. Item 3
   * Item 3a
   * Item 3b</pre>
    </div>
    <div class="col">
      <h3>Miscellaneous</h3>
      <p>Images</p>
      <pre>
![GitHub Logo](/images/logo.png)
Format: ![Alt Text](url)
</pre>
     <p>Links</p>
     <pre>
http://github.com - automatic!
[GitHub](http://github.com)</pre>
<p>Blockquotes</p>
     <pre>
As Kanye West said:

</pre>
